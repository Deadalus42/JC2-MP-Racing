Features
- Spawn/delete checkpoints.
- Very easily move checkpoints around and switch their order.
- On-screen controls.
- Multiple players at once.
- Seperate world for each editing instance.
- Instant playtesting, a la Trackmania.
- Instant previewing of starting grids.


Specifics
- Command: /raceeditor
- Server is instanced a RaceEditor class, player is teleported to another world, then their client is instanced a RaceEditor class.
